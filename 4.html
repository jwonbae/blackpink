ABOUT ROSE

BIOGRAPHY

Roseanne Park (born 11 February 1997), better known by the mononym Rosé (Korean: 로제), is a South Korean-New Zealand singer based in South Korea.[3][1] Born in New Zealand and raised in Australia, Rosé signed with South Korean label YG Entertainment following an audition in 2012, training there for four years. She made her debut as a vocalist in the girl group Blackpink in August 2016 and made her solo debut with her single album R in March 2021.


FASHION

in 2020, Rosé was named the global ambassador for Yves Saint Laurent by Anthony Vaccarello, becoming its first global ambassador in 59 years.[31] She was the global face for Saint Laurent's Fall 2020 campaign.[32] In 2021, Rosé became the muse for luxury cosmetics brand Yves Saint Laurent Beauté.


R

On 2 June 2020, it was announced that Rosé would debut solo following the release of Blackpink's first Korean language full length album.[17][18] On 30 December 2020, in an interview with South Korean media outlet Osen, Rosé revealed that filming for her debut music video would begin in mid-January 2021.[19] On 26 January 2021, a promotional teaser of Rosé's solo debut was released, revealing that a preview of her solo debut would be revealed through Blackpink Livestream Concert: The Show on 31 January 2021.[20][21] Shortly after the performance, Rosé’s solo, titled "Gone" gained positive feedback from Korean news website My Daily.

Rosé's debut single album, entitled R, was released on 12 March 2021.[23] Upon release, with 39 million views in 24 hours on her lead single On the Ground, she currently holds the title as the most viewed Korean music video of a soloist in 24 hours after breaking the almost 8-year record of former labelmate Psy, Gentleman.
